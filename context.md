# Product Context

## What is Infineo?

Infineo is a Disney/Pixar-inspired mythology storytelling platform for children aged 5–12 years.

The platform uses stories from Ancient India to help children develop:

* Confidence
* Empathy
* Emotional Intelligence
* Resilience
* Values
* Critical Thinking

The experience should feel magical, cinematic, immersive, and story-driven.

Avoid making the website feel like a generic EdTech product or SaaS dashboard.

---

## Target Audience

Primary:

* Parents

Secondary:

* Teachers
* Children

---

## Primary Conversion Goal

BOOK A DEMO CLASS

Every design, UX, content, animation, and layout decision should support this goal.

If a tradeoff exists between visual decoration and conversion, prioritize conversion.

---

## Mascot Rules

Neo is the official Infineo mascot.

Neo acts as:

* Story guide
* Companion
* Friendly assistant

Neo is a core part of the brand identity and should not be removed from major experiences unless explicitly requested.

---

## Section Journey

The intended landing page flow is:

1. Title (Sticky Header)
2. Hero
3. BroadwayText
4. Impact
5. Parents
6. Modules
7. DemoClass
8. Pricing
9. Footer

Floating elements:

* FloatingChatbot
* FloatingWhatsapp

Do not rearrange sections without explicit approval.

---

## BroadwayText

BroadwayText exists for storytelling and emotional engagement.

It is not a CTA section.

It is not a feature section.

It is a cinematic text experience between major sections.

---

## Chatbot Rules

Current chatbot is a scripted FAQ assistant.

It is not an AI chatbot.

It is not connected to an LLM.

Do not replace with OpenAI, Gemini, LangChain, RAG, or other AI solutions unless explicitly requested.

Primary purpose:

* Answer parent questions
* Reduce friction
* Increase demo bookings

---

## Demo Booking

Booking form submissions should be delivered via EmailJS.

Recipient email:

[infineo@infineo.live](mailto:infineo@infineo.live)

Do not hardcode alternative email addresses.

---

## Theme Requirements

Both themes are first-class experiences.

* Light Mode must remain fully functional.
* Dark Mode must remain fully functional.

A feature is not complete unless it works correctly in both themes.

---

## Mythology Assets

Mythological characters are visual storytelling assets.

There is currently no curriculum mapping between specific gods and specific learning modules.

Do not assume associations such as:

* Krishna → Leadership
* Hanuman → Courage
* Ganesha → Wisdom

unless explicitly introduced later.

---

## Animation Philosophy

Desired feeling:

* Magical
* Disney-like
* Pixar-inspired
* Storybook
* Cinematic

Avoid:

* Aggressive parallax
* Excessive motion
* Flashy startup animations
* Cyberpunk aesthetics
* Generic SaaS animations

Animations should support storytelling rather than distract from it.
